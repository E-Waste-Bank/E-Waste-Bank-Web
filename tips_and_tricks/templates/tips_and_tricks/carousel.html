🍃 E-WASTE 🍃

TIPS AND TRICKS

Tips for Managing e-Waste


1. Buy Less

2. Organize what you have.

3. Give away or donate your e-waste.

4. Sell

How to Reduce e-Waste ?


💫 Be a good consumer. Do some research when you’re ready to buy a new product.

💫 Consider limiting the number of electronics you own.

💫 Reuse as often as possible.

💫 Teach kids about e-waste.

💫 Recycle, recycle, and recyle
